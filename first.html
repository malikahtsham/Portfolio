Why is link building important for SEO?

If you want your website to rank higher on Google, link building can help you achieve that. By building links, you can increase your online visibility and authority. Link building is particularly important if you want your brand or product to rank high in search engines because it can generate more visible results than other SEO strategies. However, it's important to note that quality matters more than quantity when it comes to link building. Therefore, it's crucial to focus on building high-quality links rather than just focusing on the number of links.

What is white hat (organic) and black hat link building?

Seeders only provide white hat link building services for its clients, which means that we follow Google's guidelines and set up strategies that are ethical and legitimate. White hat link building is focused on improving the content quality and addressing the needs of the website visitors. In contrast, black hat link building techniques violate Google's guidelines, and if Google identifies such practices, it may result in penalties. At Seeders, we are committed to protecting our clients from such penalties and ensuring that they receive the best possible services.
